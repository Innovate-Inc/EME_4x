EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 2 - Distribution

Mandatory element

EPA Requirement/Guidance

This section is required by EPA to ensure proper distribution and discovery of the resource. This section is designed to support resources that are available from either a single distributor or multiple distributors. For a given Distributor, provide a single Contact and then populate corresponding sub elements (Digital Transfer Options, Standard Order Process, and Distribution Format) that pertain to that particular contact. Any given distributor could potentially have several formats available and several URLs from which to obtain the resource. This structure helps ensure that each method of obtaining the resource is directly associated with a point of contact. At a minimum, one contact and one digital transfer option are required.

The required elements for Contact and Contact Details are: Role, Organization, and E-mail.

The required elements for Digital Transfer Options are: Linkage and Function.

Distribution Information Section

  • Distribution Contact - The person or organization that should be contacted regarding accessing the data resource.
  • Digital Transfer Options - Information about the technical means and media by which a resource is obtained from the distributor.
  • Standard Order Process - The process by which the resource was obtained or received and other related instructions or fee information
  • Distribution Format - Description of the computer language construct that specifies the representation of data objects in a record, file, message, storage device, or transmission channel
