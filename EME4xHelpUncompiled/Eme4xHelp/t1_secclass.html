EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 1

Element: Security Classification
Type: text
Short Name: secclass

FGDC Definition

Handling restrictions imposed on the data set because of national security, privacy, or other concerns.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

This element is required for minimum EPA compliancy so that assets may be properly categorized with respect to sharing with external agencies and the general public. Choose one of the following options for categorizing your data in order to reflect the organizations with whom the information may be shared.
No Confidentiality = Publicly available
Medium Confidentiality = May be shared with trusted partners
High Confidentiality = Internal EPA Only
