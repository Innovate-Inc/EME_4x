Element: North Bounding Coordinate
Type: real
Short Name: northbc

FGDC Definition

Northern-most coordinate of the limit of coverage expressed in latitude.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

This field is required for EPA geospatial assets as per minimum FGDC standards. Follow FGDC guidance and domain information provided below for implementation of this field.

Domain

-90.0 <= North Bounding Coordinate <= 90.0
