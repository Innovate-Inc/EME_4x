Element: Standard Order Process
Type: compound MD_StandardOrderProcess
ISO Element Name: distributionOrderProcess

EPA Requirement/Guidance

The process in which the resource is obtained or received and other related instructions or fee information. The following elements are included in this section:
  • fees - Fees and terms for obtaining the data/dataset. Best Practices: Include at the end the monetary unit alphabetic code as specified in ISO 4217. Common codes: United States = USD, Canada = CAD, Mexico = MXN or MXV. Example: 45.00USD
  • plannedAvailableDateTime - Date and time the resource will be available.
  • orderingInstructions - General instructions, terms, and services provided by the data distributor.
  • turnaround - Typical time required for filing a data request. Domain: free text
