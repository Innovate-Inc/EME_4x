Element: West Bounding Coordinate
Type: text
Short Name: westbc

FGDC Definition

Western-most coordinate of the limit of coverage expressed in longitude.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

Required for EPA geospatial assets as per minimum FGDC standards. Follow FGDC guidance for implementation of this field.

Domain
