EPA Requirement/Guidance

Inclusion of one or more ISO keywords is required for minimum EPA compliancy in order to comply with inter-Agency sharing initiatives. The ISO 19115 Topic Category is a general categorization of data sets that is intended to provide data classification consistency across agencies. Select one or more of the basic ISO keywords for a general classification of your data or application.

Data.gov Requirement/Guidance

Mandatory Tags (or keywords) help users discover your resource; please include terms that would be used by technical and non-technical users.

ISO Definition

High-level geographic data thematic classification to assist in the grouping and search of available geographic data sets.

FGDC Definition

Words describing key aspects of the geospatial asset.
