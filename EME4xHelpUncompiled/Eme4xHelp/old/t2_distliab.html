EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 2

Element: Distribution Liability
Type: text
Short Name: distliab

FGDC Definition

Statement of the liability assumed by the distributor.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

The use of distribution liability is required for minimum EPA compliancy, using 1 of 2 standard disclaimers depending on the nature of the data set: freely available, or restricted by license agreements. If data are obtained from third parties, retain original distribution liability along with EPA standard disclaimers if they exist.
