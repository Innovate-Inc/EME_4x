EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 1

Element: Range of Dates
Type: compound (beginning date and end date)
Short Name: rngdates

FGDC Definition

Means of encoding a range of dates and times.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

Use the range of dates option for documenting the time period information when the information in your resource reflects a range of dates. Each date in the series may include the day, month and year, or just the month and year, or just the year. Dates are documented using the standard date format (YYYY, YYYYMM, or YYYYMMDD, where Y=year, M=month, D=day [no slashes are permitted]).

In order to enter this into the EME, enter the beginning date and end date separated by a hyphen (e.g., 20080808 - 20081008). In cases where the resource contains information that is reflective of a single date only or multiple individual dates, use the single date/multiple dates option.
