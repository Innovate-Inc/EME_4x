EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 1

Element: Single Date (Calendar Date)
Type: compound (date)
Short Name: sngdate (caldate)

FGDC Definition

Means of encoding a single date and time. The year (and optionally month, or month and day) of the resource.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

Use the single date option for documenting the time period information when the resource reflects a single date (this may be a singe date, a single month and year, or a single year). Dates are documented using the standard date format (YYYY, YYYYMM, or YYYYMMDD, where Y=year, M=month, D=day [no slashes are permitted]). In cases where the resource contains information that is reflective of multiple dates or a range of dates, use the multiple date/range of dates option.

Domain

  • free date
Note: Date information posted within this field should be documented using the standard date format (YYYY, YYYYMM, or YYYYMMDD, where Y=year, M=month, D=day [no slashes are permitted]).
