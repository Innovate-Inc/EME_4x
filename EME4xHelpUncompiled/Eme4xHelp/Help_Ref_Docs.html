Reference Information

Data.gov

Data.gov is the home of the U.S. Government’s open data. You can find Federal, state and local data, tools, and resources to conduct research, build apps, design data visualizations, and more.
Project Open Data is a collection of code, tools, and case studies to help agencies adopt the U.S. Government's Open Data Policy and unlock the potential of government data. The site includes the official governance, policy documents, reporting requirements, and schema definition.

EPA Environmental Dataset Gateway

The EPA Environmental Dataset Gateway (EDG) is the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency’s (EPA) central access point for geospatial resources and datasets on the Web. OEI manages the EDG in partnership with data stewards within the regions and program offices.

EPA Geospatial Metadata Technical Specification

EPA has developed Agency guidelines for publishing geospatial metadata for data sets, applications, and services. For more information about the EPA Geospatial Metadata Technical Specification, please visit http://epa.gov/geospatial/policies.html.

ISO 19115 - Metadata for Geographic Data

ISO 19115 is the international standard for metadata for geographic data, and is officially endorsed by the Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC). The links below provide additional resources for understanding ISO.
FGDC Guidance on Geospatial Metadata Standards and the transition from CSDGM to ISO
NOAA Wiki that allows interactive navigation through the structure of ISO 19115
Comprehensive NOAA guide to implementing ISO 19115 (PDF) (348pp, 5.1MB)

FGDC Content Standard for Digital Geospatial Metadata (CSDGM)

For additional information on the Federal Geographic Data Committee's (FGDC) Content Standard for Digital Geospatial Metadata (CSDGM), please visit FGDC CSDGM.
