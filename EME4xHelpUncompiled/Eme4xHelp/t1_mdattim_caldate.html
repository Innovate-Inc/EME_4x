EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 1

Element: Multiple Dates (Multiple Single Dates)
Type: compound (multiple single dates)
Short Name: mdattim

FGDC Definition

Means of encoding multiple individual dates and times.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

Use the multiple dates option for documenting the time period information when the resource reflects a series of individual dates. Each date in the series may include the day, month and year, or just the month and year, or just the year. Dates are documented using the standard date format (YYYY, YYYYMM, or YYYYMMDD, where Y=year, M=month, D=day [no slashes are permitted]).

In order to enter this into the EME, enter each individual date separated by commas (e.g., 20080808, 20080908, 20081008). In cases where the resource contains information that is reflective of a single date only or a range of dates, use the single date/range of dates option.
