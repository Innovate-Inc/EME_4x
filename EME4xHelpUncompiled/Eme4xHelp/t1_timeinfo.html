EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 1

Element: Time Period Information
Type: compound (single date, or multiple dates, or range of dates)
Short Name: timeinfo

FGDC Definition

Information about the date and time of an event.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

This element is required for EPA geospatial assets as per minimum FGDC standards. Users may choose to enter a single date, a range of dates, or multiple dates depending on the time period of the data set/resource. Use the single date option for documenting the time period information when the resource reflects a single date. Use the range of dates option for documenting the time period information when the information in your resource reflects a range of dates. Use the multiple dates option for documenting the time period information when the resource reflects a series of individual dates.

To document the date information using the EME, a user would enter the information using one of the following options:

  1.  Single Date: enter a single date (e.g., 20080812)
  2.  Range of Dates: enter start and end dates separated by a hyphen (e.g., 20080812 - 20080824)
  3.  Multiple Dates: enter each date separated by commas (e.g., 20080812, 20080818, 20080824)

Domain

  • free date
Note: Date information posted within this field should be documented using the standard date format (YYYY, YYYYMM, or YYYYMMDD, where Y=year, M=month, D=day [no slashes are permitted]), or it may be in the form of free text, such as 'Unknown'.
