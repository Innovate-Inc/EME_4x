EPA Metadata Editor (EME)

Interface Element Description: Tab 1

Element: Calendar Date
Type: date
Short Name: caldate

FGDC Definition

The year (and optionally month, or month and day) of the resource.

EPA Requirement/Guidance

This element is required for EPA geospatial assets as per minimum FGDC standards. Follow FGDC guidance and information provided below for implementation of this field.

Domain

  • "Unknown"
  • free date
Note: Date information posted within this field should be documented using the standard date format (YYYY, YYYYMM, or YYYYMMDD, where Y=year, M=month, D=day [no slashes are permitted]), or it may be in the form of free text, such as 'Unknown'.
